Richárd Csáky

Master's student at BME

My research interests are Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and how AI and neuroscience could inspire each other to build safe AGI.

My M.S. studies are in Computer Science under Gábor Recski.
Previously, at BME I graduated with a highest honours B.S. in Mechatronics Engineering.

In the spring semester of 2019/2020 I will be at KU Leuven on Erasmus.
